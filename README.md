# Jahreszeiten-Simulator (Season Simulator)

A Python-based simple interactive tool to visualize the Earth's orbit around the Sun and understand how seasons change across both hemispheres.

![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-blue.svg)
![Python](https://img.shields.io/badge/python-3.x-green.svg)

## Description
This tool provides a simplified 3D/2D visualization of the Earth's movement around the Sun every year. It helps users visualize the tilt of the Earth's axis and how it affects sunlight distribution throughout the year - resulting in the seasons.

> **Note:** The current interface is in German only. I will cover English language support soon!

## Key Features
- **Dynamic Orbit:** Watch the Earth move around the Sun in real-time.
- **Velocity Control:** Adjust the speed of both Earth's rotation and its orbital movement.
- **Reference Lines:** Toggle visibility for the Equator, Tropics, and Arctic Circles.
- **Dual Perspective:** 
  - **Sun-centered view:** See the whole solar system layout.
  - **Earth-centered view:** Click on the Earth to zoom in and see the sunlight distribution up close.
- **Educational Pop-ups:** Information appears automatically when the Earth reaches solstices or equinoxes.
- **Hemisphere Toggle:** Switch descriptions to focus on either the Northern or Southern Hemisphere perspective.
